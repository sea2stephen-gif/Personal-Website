~/wgu/capstone/smb-network-design
CAPSTONE ● COMPLETED

Small Office Network Design & Deployment

Designed and built a segmented enterprise network for a fictional financial consulting firm using Cisco IOS on 2911 routers and 2960 switches. Architected the network around four VLANs with router-on-a-stick inter-VLAN routing, then layered on extended ACLs to enforce departmental isolation while permitting DHCP relay traffic to a centralized server. Hardened the topology with RSTP redundancy, sticky-MAC port security, AUP login banners, and Syslog/NTP centralization. Documented the intended IPSec site-to-site VPN configuration alongside the platform's IOS limitations. Diagnosed and resolved real issues during build, including an ACL that silently blocked DHCP DISCOVER broadcasts and switches that couldn't reach Syslog without a management SVI and default gateway. Validated against 10 test cases (7 predefined, 3 custom).
